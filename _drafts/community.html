Community

Join a like-minded community where we learn about technology and be productive together.

We all know that career transition into tech is an uphill battle. Even with a background in medical imaging where the feild is constantly evolving, I already found it challenging to keep up with technology and latest software updates. I created this platform to build a community and include people who are considering joining tech sectors through skills of technical writing and programming.

It would also be nice to share with you my career journey and life experiences so that we can support each other in the process of growth. As a first generation immagrant who left Taiwan and came to the UK alone without support from family, I have been through various setbacks, challenges and rejections. Knowing that my experience and help others is truly rewarding to me. So let's connect!
